This website showcases the work I have produced in the visual arts as a practicing artist, alongside the work created by my students through my teaching. The conceptual frameworks that guide both my teaching and my artistic practice have been developed entirely by myself. Under my guidance, the work produced through teaching reflects the creativity and efforts of my students while the work presented as part of my own visual arts practice is conceptualized and created solely by myself.
